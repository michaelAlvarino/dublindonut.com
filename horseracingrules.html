GUIDE FOR ULTIMATE EXCITEMENT AND FUN

Rules

This is a pretty standard drinking game. You bet on a horse. If your horse wins, you give drinks. If your horse loses, you drink.

  • 1st Place gives 3 drinks
  • 2nd Place gives 2 drinks
  • 3rd Place gives 1 drink
  • 4th Place drinks 1

BONUS: If the order of the horses is Horse 1, Horse 2, Horse 3, Horse 4, everyone drinks 1
